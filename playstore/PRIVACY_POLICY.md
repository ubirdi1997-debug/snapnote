# Privacy Policy for SnapNote Voice

**Last Updated:** December 27, 2024

## Introduction

MINORMEND CONSTRUCTION PRIVATE LIMITED ("we", "our", or "us") operates the SnapNote Voice mobile application (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service.

## Our Commitment to Privacy

SnapNote Voice is designed with privacy as a core principle. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information remains secure.

## Data Collection and Storage

### What We Do NOT Collect

SnapNote Voice is a fully offline application. We do NOT collect, transmit, or store:

- Personal information
- Usage data
- Device information
- Location data
- Contact information
- Any analytics or tracking data
- Any data that leaves your device

### What We Store Locally

All data created using SnapNote Voice is stored exclusively on your device:

- **Notes Content**: All notes you create (text, voice transcriptions, scanned text) are stored locally on your device using local database storage (Hive).
- **App Settings**: Theme preferences and app settings are stored locally on your device.

**Important**: All data remains on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third-party services.

## Permissions

SnapNote Voice requests the following permissions:

### Microphone Permission
- **Purpose**: To record voice notes and convert speech to text
- **Usage**: Used only when you actively use the voice recording feature
- **Data Handling**: Audio is processed locally on your device using offline speech recognition. No audio data is transmitted or stored externally.

### Camera Permission
- **Purpose**: To capture images for text extraction using OCR (Optical Character Recognition)
- **Usage**: Used only when you actively use the camera scanning feature
- **Data Handling**: Images are processed locally on your device using offline OCR. Images are temporarily stored only for processing and are immediately deleted after text extraction.

## Third-Party Services

SnapNote Voice uses the following offline-capable services that do not transmit data:

- **Google ML Kit Text Recognition**: Used for offline OCR text extraction. All processing occurs locally on your device.
- **Speech-to-Text Services**: Uses device's built-in offline speech recognition capabilities.

**No data is shared with these services or transmitted over the internet.**

## Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

- Your data is protected by your device's security measures
- We recommend using device-level encryption and screen locks
- You can delete all app data by uninstalling the application

## Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since we do not collect any data, this is inherently protected.

## Data Deletion

You can delete all your notes and app data at any time by:

1. Deleting individual notes within the app
2. Clearing app data through your device settings
3. Uninstalling the application

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
